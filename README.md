# E-Commerce-Business-Intelligence-and-Performance-Analytics-Using-SQL



Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze e-commerce transactional data using SQL in order to extract meaningful business insights. The analysis focuses on understanding revenue performance, customer behavior, product trends, and regional sales distribution. The goal is to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights that support data-driven business decisions.
Problem Statement
E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of data from daily transactions, but this data is often underutilized due to lack of structured analysis. Key challenges include difficulty in identifying top-performing products, understanding customer purchasing behavior, tracking revenue trends over time, and evaluating regional performance. Without proper analysis, businesses risk poor decision-making, missed revenue opportunities, and weak customer retention strategies.
Impact and Business Value
This project provides a structured approach to analyzing business performance using SQL, enabling organizations to derive actionable insights from raw data. It helps identify high-value customers, improve product and category performance, and understand revenue trends across time and regions. The segmentation of customers supports targeted marketing strategies, while trend and growth analysis improves forecasting and planning. Overall, this project enhances decision-making efficiency, improves business visibility, and supports long-term revenue optimization.
